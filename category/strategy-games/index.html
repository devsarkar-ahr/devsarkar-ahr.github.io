Strategy Games 

Strategy games focus on planning, decision-making, and smart resource management. Instead of relying on fast reactions, these games challenge players to think ahead, analyze situations, and choose the best approach to win or progress.

Because many strategy challenges are completed in short stages or rounds, they work well for users who enjoy thinking-based gameplay without committing to long sessions.
