Puzzle Games

Puzzle games are designed to test thinking, logic, and problem-solving skills through simple but engaging challenges. Instead of relying on speed or fast reactions, these games focus on planning, pattern recognition, and decision-making. Each level or challenge encourages players to think carefully and find the most effective solution.

Because puzzle games are easy to understand and usually built around short objectives, they are well suited for quick play sessions without long time commitments.
