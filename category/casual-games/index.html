Casual Games 

Casual games are simple, easy-to-play games that focus on quick entertainment without complex controls or long learning time. These games are designed for short sessions and can be enjoyed by players of all skill levels. Most casual games offer instant gameplay and clear objectives, making them perfect for quick relaxation.

Because they do not require long commitment, Casual Games are ideal for users who want light entertainment during short free moments.
